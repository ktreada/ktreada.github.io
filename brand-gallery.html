Logos

I worked with the client to develop a logo
that was "cool and sleek", as she stated in her initial proposal.
Because the client is involved in data analytics, she wanted a logo
that conveyed the sense of "finding gold nuggets" of information
within the grey areas of information.

Business Cards

The client wanted business cards designed to
display her personality as well as convey important information.
Ultimately, these cards were designed to stand out and were created
to be used with metallic foil, if the client chooses to print them as such.

Final Website

The creation of a landing page was crucial to the
client's startup, as she is starting a tech company and needs to have
a functional website that subtly displays her skills and assets.
Vector artwork was used in place of photographs because of the lack
of content, but the client resolved to build the website herself and
insert her content later.
